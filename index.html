BUSINESS BLOG / DOMINIK SAMOCIUK

Dominik Samociuk

Information Security Manager at Future Processing

Dominik started as an independent security researcher and went through variety of roles in his professional carrier. Combining his professional work with the constant search for new knowledge, he received a PhD in computer science. He is an author or co-author of more than 20 scientific as well as popular science articles and a holder of various certifications, e.g. CISSP, OSCP. He's constantly finding new challenges.
